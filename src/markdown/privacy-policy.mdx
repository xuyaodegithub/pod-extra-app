This information is used to enhance the Service and understand user behavior.

This helps us ensure the proper functioning of the Service and address any technical issues.

### 3.3 Audio Data
With your permission, we may collect audio data from podcast episodes for the purpose of generating summaries. This data is analyzed by our AI system and not retained. We only use it in the moment to extract the necessary information to create accurate and useful summaries for you.

### 3.4 Information from Third Party Sources

If we decide to obtain information from third parties, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly to reflect such changes and ensure that any collection is done in line with applicable laws and with your consent, where required.

## 8. Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies
### 8.1 What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website or use our Service. They are used to remember your preferences, track your usage of the Service, and improve your overall experience.

### 8.2 The Cookies We Use
We use the following types of cookies:
- **Essential Cookies**: These cookies are necessary for the basic functioning of the Service, such as allowing you to log in, access secure areas, and navigate through the website. Without these cookies, the Service may not be able to provide you with the full range of features.
- **Functionality Cookies**: These cookies enable us to remember your choices and preferences, such as your preferred language, podcast settings, or display options. They help us personalize your experience with the Service.
- **Analytics Cookies**: We use analytics cookies to collect information about how users interact with the Service, such as the number of visitors, page views, and user behavior patterns. This information is used to improve the Service and understand user needs.
- **Advertising Cookies**: We may use advertising cookies to display relevant ads to you based on your interests and usage of the Service. These cookies are used in conjunction with third-party advertising partners to target ads more effectively.

### 8.3 How to Disable Cookies
You can usually disable cookies through your browser settings. However, please be aware that disabling cookies may affect your ability to use the Service fully, as some features may rely on cookies to function properly.
